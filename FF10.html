My name is Milo!

Hello!

My name is Janelle, And right here with me is my hero, my pet, and most of all. My best friend Milo. I wasn't really fond of animals, led alone an animal to own. But one day, my mom had gifted me a lifelong companion. I was quite hesitant to accept this gift but as time went by, Milo really started to grow on me.

There were dark times in my life and when no one was there to comfort me, somehow Milo was able to reciprocate with my feelings. I thought it was just coincidence. I mean, come on. A dog understanding human feelings? But in reality I'm the one being ridiculous. In the middle of my breakdown, Milo was there to comfort me. My initial reaction was to avoid her, but her warmth made me feel safe. Ever since then, I wonder how much more difficult life could have been if Milo was never a part of my life.
