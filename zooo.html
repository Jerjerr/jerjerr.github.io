Cockfighting!

The first entry we have here are two chickens who are equipped and ready for battle! Our neighbor loves to breed and produce chickens specifically for "Cockfighting" (locally termed sabong.)

Fufu, the community cat!

Though Fufu acts as the community cat, it's almost fictional how this car operates. It loves to go around town and is familiar to everyone. One reason why the community loves this cat is because of how diligent and clingy it can be. We aren't necessarily afraid to pet it since cats are one of the few types of animals capable of cleaning themselves.
